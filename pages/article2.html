Students Share Their Time in Doing Community Service


“The spirit of service is the heart of humanity.” This is a beautiful quote by Lailah Akira. Imagine a community where each person cares only for himself- no sharing, loving, caring, or giving! It’s hard to imagine that such a place could exist. To live is to give and to serve, this is such a Christian ethic.


At Victory Baptist Academy, the Grade 10 students have their own form of community service. As one of the requirements for them to graduate, Sir Dennis Jardinez unselfishly devote his time in supervising them in their Citizenship Advancement Training (C.A.T.). Each student has to finish 36 hours by actively participating incommunity activities. This aims the enhancement of the students’ social responsibility and commitment to the development of their communities.


This activity started first with lectures during the first weeks. Now, the Grade 10 students are participating in cleaning the school and later on extending this project to a specific area or a barangay. Because of instilling the awareness of this social responsibility , the students are looking forward to wider opportunity of sharing their love to their community and eventually their country.


-by Gwyneth Faye N. Domoguen
