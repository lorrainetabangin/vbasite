Zenith


We now humbly present to you Zenith on its 4th publication! We are thankful for another opportunity to conceptualize and put into writing another issue of this school organ. We want to express our gratitude to the school principal, Ma’am Nancy Culay-an for granting us the use of the facilities, resources, and equipments of the school. We would also like to thank Sir Jefrey Angelo Espelico and Sir Jim Steven Espelico for using their skills, time, and effort to edit, layout, and design our school paper. To the grade 9 and 10 students, we thank you for your support and contributions for this school project. We would also like to express our heartfelt gratitude to all the sponsors; you are indeed a great blessing to the school. We especially thank our journalism teacher, Ma’am Maryjane Cigaral; we sincerely appreciate your time and effort in guiding us in writing our articles. Again, we thank you all for making this school paper possible once again. May the good Lord continue to use people like you for the betterment of this academy.
